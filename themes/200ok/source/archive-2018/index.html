Location: Tulsa Central Library

400 Civic Center, Tulsa, OK 74103

Breakfast and Lunch provided. Additional amenities include Hearing Loop, wheel chair accessibility, and a mother's room.

Logistics

We want to make sure you have everything you need to get here and enjoy your time at 200 OK and in Tulsa.

Airport

Tulsa International Airport (TUL) is our airport if you're flying in.

Ground Transportation

Uber & Lyft are both available in Tulsa.

Hotel

If you're staying in Tulsa, Aloft Tulsa Downtown, Hampton Inn & Suites Tulsa Downtown, and the iconic Tulsa Mayo Hotel are all very close to our venue.

Parking

Parking at the Central Library's garage is limited, and pricey for all day parking. There are some surrounding lots like the Cox Business Center paid lot. This will probably have more availability and is a short walk to the Library. See this online map for a couple of parking garages nearby.

Venue

We are at the Tulsa Central Library in the Pocahontas Greadington Learning and Creativity Center (Lower Level 2).

Meals

We will provide continental-style breakfast in the morning, coffee, snacks, and drinks thru-out the day, and lunch including vegan and gluten-free options.

After-Event Venues

We will give every attendee 2 tickets for drinks at one of our after-event venues: MixCo Craft Cocktails & Speakeasy Bar or All About Cha Gourmet Coffee & Tea.

Weekend

If you can, you should consider sticking around Tulsa for the weekend. There are a few classic annual Tulsa events going on downtown:

  • Blue Dome Arts Festival for local art, music, and fun.
  • HopJam Beer & Music Festival
  • Tulsa Mayfest

Code of Conduct

Before attending the conference, please review the 200OK Code of Conduct. The CoC applies to all attendees, speakers, volunteers, and vendors at official and unofficial events by Techlahoma and any location where attendees may be congregating. Contact us if you have questions.
